# 🚀 FinovaAI — Smart Finance Intelligence Platform

> 💡 *Turn Financial Complexity into Confident Decisions*

FinovaAI is an AI-powered platform that unifies financial data—📰 news, 📑 research, and 📊 portfolio insights—into one secure ecosystem.  
It helps investors detect risks early ⚠️, verify information authenticity 🔍, and make smarter decisions in real time 🤖.

---

## 🎯 The Problem
Financial data today is:
- 🌐 Scattered across multiple platforms  
- ❌ Often unreliable or inconsistent  
- 📉 Difficult to analyze effectively  

👉 Result: Poor decisions, missed opportunities, and unmanaged risks.

---

## 💡 The Solution
FinovaAI brings everything into **one intelligent system**:
- 📊 Centralized financial data  
- 🤖 AI-powered insights  
- ⚠️ Real-time risk detection  
- 🛡️ Fraud & misinformation protection  

---

## 🧩 Core Modules

### 📊 My Portfolio (`/dashboard`)
Your financial command center:
- 💰 Track investments, P&L & portfolio health  
- 📰 Get portfolio-specific news insights  
- ⚠️ AI-based **Future Risk Predictability**  
- 📩 **SMS Alerts** for instant risk notifications  
- 🎯 Personalized investment strategies  

---

### 📰 Market Pulse (`/dashboard/news`)
Your smart news engine:
- 🏛️ Regulatory updates  
- 📑 Research reports  
- 📈 Market news & trends  
- 🧠 Institutional & individual insights  

👉 Filter what matters. Ignore the noise.

---

### ⚠️ Truth Agent (`/dashboard/true`)
Your financial truth detector:
- 🚨 Detect scams & fraud  
- 🧾 Verify news credibility  
- 🛑 Avoid misinformation-driven losses  

---

### 🤖 FinPilot AI (`/dashboard/bot`)
Your AI financial assistant:
- 💬 Ask anything about markets or portfolio  
- 📂 Upload PDFs (reports, statements)  
- 🔍 Analyze any portfolio instantly  
- 🧠 Get smart insights & explanations  

---

### ⚙️ Ingest Settings (`/dashboard/ingest`)
Control your financial data:
- 🧾 Upload portfolio documents  
- 🎯 Set risk appetite & strategy  
- ✏️ Update holdings manually  
- 🔄 Customize investment preferences
